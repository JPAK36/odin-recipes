Hydrating Soup

A soup for both humans and animals to enjoy!

This is a little recipe I put together myself. I've been making it daily for as long as I can remember and the best thing is it never gets old! It is especially tasty on a hot summer day, so be sure to pack plenty for your next beach day! And without further ado here is the recipe:

  • Hydrogen
  • Oxygen

That's it! You don't need anything else. One crucial point to remember is that it's important to maintain a strict 2:1 ratio of hydrogen to oxygen. Other than that, this is really all you need for this amazing dish!

If you're feeling adventurous, feel free to add some ice cubes to taste. In all my years of making this dish, I have yet to meet someone who didn’t enjoy it. Some have asked about adding salt—and while you can, I usually advise against it unless you're preparing it for your fishy friends.
